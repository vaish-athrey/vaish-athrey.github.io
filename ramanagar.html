As part of the Ramanagar Program, we undertook a comprehensive project aimed at enhancing the educational experience for 3rd-grade students studying Environmental Science (EVS). Our team developed engaging video content with voiceovers based on the prescribed study material for chapters of the EVS curriculum. To make learning visually enriching, we created detailed charts on various topics, providing a lasting resource that would benefit not only the current students but also future batches. Additionally, we designed interactive PowerPoint presentations to simplify complex concepts and developed AR-VR videos that showcased plant varieties, fostering a deeper understanding of the natural world. These innovative videos were designed as conversation-style content to make learning more impactful, encouraging students to adopt a balanced diet by connecting it with relatable examples.

To further enhance creative learning, we designed activity sheets that made the educational process fun and engaging for students. The highlight of the project was a working model of a Greenhouse Management System, which visually demonstrated the importance of greenhouses and their role in environmental sustainability. Complementing the model, we created an educational video explaining greenhouse gases and their significance. By incorporating hands-on models and visual aids, this project aimed to inspire curiosity and provide students with a holistic and interactive approach to learning about their environment.
